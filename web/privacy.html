Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for Image Converter. This document outlines how we collect, use, and protect any information that may be gathered when you use our application.

Information Collection and Use

Image Converter is a desktop application that runs entirely on your local machine. We do not collect any personal information from you when you use our application.

  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, images, or file information to external servers.
  • Local Storage Only: All application data, including profiles, settings, and processed file records, are stored locally on your device.
  • No Internet Requirement: The application does not require internet access to function properly.

Image Processing

All image processing occurs locally on your device. The application:

  • Reads images from folders you specify
  • Processes these images according to your settings
  • Saves the processed images to a destination folder you choose
  • Keeps a local record of processed files to prevent duplicate processing

At no point are your images uploaded to any server or shared with third parties.

Automatic Updates

If you choose to enable automatic updates, the application may check for new versions. This involves connecting to our update server, but no personal information is transmitted during this process beyond what is necessary to deliver the update.

Third-Party Libraries

Our application uses the following third-party libraries:

  • PyQt6 for the user interface
  • Pillow (PIL) for image processing
  • SQLite for local database storage

These libraries operate within the application environment and do not transmit data externally.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
